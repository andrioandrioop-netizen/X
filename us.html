About Us

Neards.Hub is a passion project born out of our journey in B.Sc.IT. Our mission is to archive the programs and projects we build across semesters, creating a centralized, accessible space for quick revision. By storing our source code directly on the platform, we've made our learning history easily accessible to anyone with the link.

We are continuously building and have exciting features planned for the near future. Thank you for visiting our archive, and we appreciate your patience as we grow!
